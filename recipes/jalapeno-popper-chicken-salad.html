Jalapeno Popper Chicken Salad

```

A creamy, spicy chicken salad inspired by jalapeno poppers, made with rotisserie chicken, Greek yogurt, bacon, and Monterey Jack cheese. Makes 6 servings of about 1 cup each. Great served in lettuce wraps or with jalapeno popper Popcorners.

Yield: 6 servings (1 cup each)

Ingredients

  • 1 whole rotisserie chicken, deboned and finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup plain nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Hellman's light mayo
  • 5 pieces center-cut bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 4 oz Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 3 large jalapenos, diced
  • 1/4 cup Hidden Valley ranch seasoning
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Garlic powder, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy, then crumble and set aside.
  2. Debone the rotisserie chicken and chop the meat finely.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the chopped chicken, Greek yogurt, and light mayo. Mix well.
  4. Add the crumbled bacon, shredded Monterey Jack cheese, chopped green onions, and diced jalapenos. Stir to combine.
  5. Add the ranch seasoning and season to taste with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Mix until evenly combined.
  6. Serve in lettuce wraps or with jalapeno popper Popcorners on the side.

Nutrition

Per serving: 275 calories | Protein: 25g

```
