Tzatziki Chicken Salad

A high-protein, low-carb meal prep chicken salad using Greek yogurt in place of mayo. Makes 5 cups total — macros listed per cup.

Yield: 5 cups (nutrition per 1 cup)

```

Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups chopped chicken breast (rotisserie, boiled, or canned)
  • 3/4 cup plain 0% Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped red onion
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tbsp dill
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a large bowl.
  2. Mix together until fully combined.

Nutrition

Per 1 cup: 241 calories | Protein: 46g | Carbs: 3g | Fat: 5g

```
