Miso Peanut Spicy Chicken Noodles

```

High-protein udon noodle bowl with a velvety miso peanut sauce, spicy gochujang chicken mince, and a fresh herb salad. Quick enough for a weeknight, impressive enough to share.

2 servings

Ingredients

Miso Peanut Sauce

  • 40g peanut butter
  • 25g white miso
  • 20ml rice vinegar
  • 20ml soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp MSG (optional)
  • 65ml water

Herb Salad

  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 10g fresh coriander
  • 4 spring onions

Spicy Chicken

  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • 1 tbsp Korean chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 400g chicken mince
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

To Serve

  • 200g udon noodles

Instructions

  1. Make the miso peanut sauce: whisk together the peanut butter, white miso, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, and MSG until smooth. Slowly drizzle in the water while whisking until the sauce is velvety and pourable. Set aside.
  2. Make the herb salad: slice the cucumber into thin half moons, pick the coriander leaves, and finely slice the green tops of the spring onions. Combine in a bowl. Chill in iced water if you want more crunch. Reserve the white parts of the spring onions for the chicken.
  3. Make the chili sauce: add the garlic, gochujang, Korean chili flakes, dark soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and the white parts of the spring onions to a blender. Blitz until smooth and set aside.
  4. Cook the chicken: heat a large frying pan over high heat. Add the oil, then the chicken mince. Press it flat and let it brown deeply on one side before breaking it up. Cook through, then pour in the chili sauce. Stir and let it bubble for 2–3 minutes until thick, sticky, and fully coating the chicken.
  5. Cook the udon noodles according to pack instructions. Drain and toss immediately with the miso peanut sauce and a splash of warm noodle water until glossy and well coated.
  6. Divide the saucy noodles between bowls. Top with the spicy chicken and the herb salad. Finish with extra miso peanut sauce if you like.

Nutrition

Per serving: 676 calories | 50g protein | 26g fat | 60g carbs

```
