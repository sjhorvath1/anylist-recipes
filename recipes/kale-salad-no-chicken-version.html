Kale Salad (No Chicken Version)

A hearty, fiber-rich kale salad with cabbage, apple, almonds, Parmesan, and a tangy Dijon honey dressing.

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

Salad

  • 1/2 lb kale
  • 1/2 lb cabbage & carrot mix, shredded
  • 35 g almonds
  • 180 g green apple, diced
  • 1/2 cup loosely shredded Parmesan
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1/8 cup honey
  • 1/2 tsp chicken bouillon

Instructions

  1. Mix salad ingredients and massage to break down kale.
  2. Whisk dressing ingredients together.
  3. Pour dressing over salad and toss.
  4. Let marinate if desired (flavor improves after several hours).

Nutrition

Calories (per serving): ~551 kcal

Protein (per serving): ~11 g

Carbohydrates (per serving): ~41 g

Fiber (per serving): ~9 g

Sugar (per serving): ~23 g

Fat (per serving): ~40 g

Total recipe (4 servings): ~2,205 kcal, ~44 g protein, ~166 g carbohydrates, ~36 g fiber, ~92 g sugar, ~162 g fat.
