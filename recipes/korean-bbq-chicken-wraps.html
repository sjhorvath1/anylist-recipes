Korean BBQ Chicken Wraps

Servings: 10 wraps

Ingredients

  • 1600g boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 3 tsp black pepper
  • 3 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 tsp onion powder
  • 100g gochujang paste
  • 50–60g minced garlic
  • 35g sesame seeds
  • 60g honey
  • 30g light soy sauce
  • 40g dark soy sauce
  • 200g low fat yogurt
  • 120g light mayo
  • 25g gochujang
  • 25g honey
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • Dash of milk
  • 300g cucumber
  • 200g grated carrots
  • 200g red onion
  • 20g sesame seeds
  • 40g rice vinegar
  • 30g sriracha
  • 10 low carb flour wraps (Mission)
  • Sesame seeds, parsley, chilli flakes (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Trim excess fat from chicken thighs. Add seasonings and marinade ingredients, mix well, and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, combine cucumber, carrots, red onion, sesame seeds, rice vinegar and sriracha. Mix until well combined.
  3. In a second bowl, combine yogurt, light mayo, gochujang, honey, garlic powder and milk. Mix until smooth.
  4. Spread marinated chicken evenly on a lined sheet pan. Bake at 200°C / 400°F for 25–30 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  5. Toast wraps lightly, slice the chicken, assemble with slaw and sauce, and serve.
