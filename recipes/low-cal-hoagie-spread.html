Low-Cal Hoagie Spread

```

A very spicy, low-calorie hoagie spread from the MG's Low Cal Sauce Series. Only 27 calories per 2 tbsp serving versus 180 calories in regular mayo. Works as a sandwich spread or salad dressing for a hoagie-themed salad.

Yield: about 1.5 cups (multiple servings)

Nutrition

Per 2 tbsp (24g) serving: 27 calories

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (125g) nonfat Greek yogurt
  • 5 tbsp (75g) honey mustard
  • 5 tbsp (75g) light mayo
  • 5 tbsp (75g) cento cherry pepper hoagie spread
  • 1 1/2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a jar (2 cups or larger), combine all ingredients and shake until fully combined. Alternatively, add all ingredients to a bowl, whisk until combined, then transfer to a jar or sauce bottle.
  2. Use as a spread on any sandwich or hoagie, or drizzle over salads for a hoagie-themed salad.
```
