High Protein Baguette Pizzas

A high-protein, low-fat twist on pizza using hollowed baguettes, marinara, mozzarella, and turkey pepperoni.

Servings: 12 pizzas

Ingredients

Base

  • 3 baguettes, cut in half lengthwise and hollowed out

Toppings

  • 1 medium jar marinara sauce (low calorie if desired)
  • 740 g low-fat mozzarella cheese (about 61 g per piece)
  • 108 slices turkey pepperoni (about 9 slices per piece)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice each baguette in half lengthwise and then along the side, then hollow out the interior to create space for toppings.
  3. Place baguette halves on a baking sheet.
  4. Spread marinara sauce evenly inside each hollowed baguette.
  5. Add mozzarella cheese evenly across each piece.
  6. Top with turkey pepperoni slices.
  7. Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 10–15 minutes.

Nutrition

Calories: 338 calories

Protein: 28 g

Fat: 11.8 g

Carbohydrates: 30 g
