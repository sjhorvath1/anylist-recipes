Crack Sauce

A bold Thai-style herb sauce blended with mint, cilantro, lime, fish sauce, and chilies. The recipe yields about 12 ounces.

Yield: About 12 ounces

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bunch mint
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 6-10 red bird chilies
  • 4 Tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 6 Tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1/4 large shallot
  • 10 cloves garlic

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until desired consistency is reached.
