Frozen Peanut Butter Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 mashed ripe banana
  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/3 cup chopped cashews
  • 8 oz enjoy life chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • Flake salt

Instructions

  1. Add the mashed banana, peanut butter, plain greek yogurt, honey, & chopped cashews into a bowl and mix until you have a sticky / smooth consistency.
  2. Plop 12 dollops onto parchment, and then lightly flatten them into a disc shape.
  3. Put in freezer for 1 hour.
  4. Before you take them out, melt your chocolate and coconut oil in the microwave until smooth.
  5. Take frozen PB bites out, and dollop a serving of melted chocolate on top, add flake salt, & set back on parchment tray.
  6. Once all are done, put back in freezer for 15–30 min.
  7. Store in freezer.
